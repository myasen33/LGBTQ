Lesbian

The sexuality "lesbian" refers to females who only feel attraction towards other females.

Pansexuality

Pansexuality is a sexuality where someone feels attraction towards any gender.

Bisexuality

Bisexuality is a sexuality where someone is attracted not exclusively to people of one particular gender.

Gay Flag

Gay is a sexuality where one only feels attraction towards those of the same gender.

Transgender

Transgender, or trans, describes a person who is a different gender than the one they were assigned at birth.

Asexual

Asexual refers to a person who does not feel sexual attraction.
